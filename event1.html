Frequently Asked Questions

Q:Does it cost anything?

A: No, our events are always free of charge.

Q:Does registering mean I am all set to attend?

A: Not yet. While your chances of getting a seat are pretty good, we will confirm attendees on a case-by-case basis to ensure we attract the right audience. Note that your seat is not confirmed until you receive a confirmation code.

Q:When will I hear back if I have a spot?

A: We'll try to confirm you at least two days before the event!

Q:Can anyone participate for the event?

A: Anyone who is passionate to learn and contribute can participate.
